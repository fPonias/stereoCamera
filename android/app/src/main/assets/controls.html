Controls
What follows is a summary of the master device controls
  1. Flip - toggle between the rear and front facing cameras
  2. Side - Specifies which side the primary device is on. Are you holding it with you left hand or right hand? Not specifying the correct side will make your image unviewable
  3. Shutter - Capture a still image
  4. Local Zoom - Adjust the digital zoom of the camera. See the Alignment section for more details.
  5. Preview - A thumbnail of the last image shot.
  6. Tilt - A meter displaying the current front to back tilt of both devices. Red is the primary device. Blue is the secondary. See the Alignment section for more details.
  7. Level - A meter displaying the current horizontal level of both devices. Red is the primary device. Blue is the secondary. See the Alignment section for more details.
