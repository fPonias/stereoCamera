Privacy Notice
Because this app accessed the device's camera and microphone, Google is requiring that we provide a privacy policy. This privacy notice discloses the privacy practices for McMunger Stereo Camera. This privacy notice applies solely to information collected by this app. It will notify you of the following:

  • What personally identifiable information is collected from you through the app, how it is used and with whom it may be shared.
  • What choices are available to you regarding the use of your data.
Information Collection, Use, and Sharing
McMunger Stereo Camera accesses image data from the device's built-in camera and audio data from the built-in microphone. Images are saved locally to the two devices used to take the image. The app itself will not distribute any images unless explicitly initiated by the user. Audio data is used for synching images and is never saved. No personally identifiable information is collected with this app, with the exception of the user taking a picture of themselves ("selfie").
Your Access to and Control Over Information
The purpose of this app is to take pictures. The user is responsible for the pictures it takes. If you point this camera at a friend or yourself, you can reasonably assume that it will produce an image in the likeness of the person you pointed it at. We are not responsible for bad pictures. The user can delete any image captures they don't like.
